Project Overview
Why Heart Disease Analysis?

Heart disease remains one of the leading causes of mortality worldwide. This project uses Tableau to transform raw health data into meaningful dashboards that highlight key risk factors and support better decision-making.

Our analysis spans demographics, lifestyle choices, clinical indicators, and regional health trends. Using interactive Tableau visualizations, we uncover hidden correlations that aid in preventive care and awareness campaigns.

Key Visualizations & Insights
👥
Gender & Age Analysis
Comparing heart disease counts across male and female patients, segmented by age categories from 18 to 80+.
🚬
Smoking & Alcohol Impact
Visualizing how smoking habits and alcohol consumption correlate with heart disease prevalence in our dataset.
🩺
Diabetic vs Stroke
Bubble chart analysis showing the intersection of diabetes status, stroke occurrence, and heart disease outcomes.
🌍
Racewise Distribution
Pie chart breakdown: White (75.57%), Black (17.81%), Hispanic (3.73%), Asian and others.
⚖️
BMI & Diabetic Correlation
Treemap and grouped bar charts showing average BMI across diabetic status and age groups, revealing obesity-diabetes links.
🏃
Physical Activity Impact
Donut chart analysis revealing how regular physical activity correlates with lower heart disease risk across the cohort.
