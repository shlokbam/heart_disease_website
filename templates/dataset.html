Dataset Information

Heart Disease Dataset

The dataset contains 4,500 patient records with 18 features covering demographics, lifestyle habits, and clinical health indicators collected for heart disease analysis.

Dataset Features

HeartDisease
Target · Binary
Whether the respondent has heart disease (Yes/No). Primary outcome variable.
BMI
Numeric · Continuous
Body Mass Index — weight-to-height ratio indicating obesity levels. Range: ~12–94.
Smoking
Binary · Lifestyle
Smoked at least 100 cigarettes in lifetime (Yes/No). Key cardiovascular risk factor.
AlcoholDrinking
Binary · Lifestyle
Heavy alcohol consumption — 14+ drinks/week for men, 7+ for women.
Stroke
Binary · Clinical
History of stroke events (Yes/No). Strong comorbidity indicator for heart disease.
PhysicalHealth
Numeric · 0–30
Days in past 30 days when physical health was not good (illness/injury).
MentalHealth
Numeric · 0–30
Days in past 30 days when mental health was not good (stress/depression).
DiffWalking
Binary · Clinical
Serious difficulty walking or climbing stairs (Yes/No). Mobility indicator.
Sex
Categorical · Demographic
Patient biological sex: Male or Female.
AgeCategory
Categorical · Demographic
Age bracket in 5-year intervals: 18–24, 25–29, ..., 80 or older.
Race
Categorical · Demographic
Ethnicity: White, Black, Hispanic, Asian, American Indian/Alaskan, Other.
Diabetic
Categorical · Clinical
Diabetes status: No, Yes, Borderline, Yes (during pregnancy).
PhysicalActivity
Binary · Lifestyle
Physical activity outside of work in past 30 days (Yes/No).
GenHealth
Ordinal · Self-reported
Self-assessed health: Excellent, Very Good, Good, Fair, Poor.
SleepTime
Numeric · Continuous
Average hours of sleep per 24-hour period. Sleep quality indicator.
Asthma
Binary · Clinical
History of asthma diagnosis (Yes/No). Comorbidity marker.
KidneyDisease
Binary · Clinical
History of kidney disease excluding kidney stones (Yes/No).
SkinCancer
Binary · Clinical
History of skin cancer diagnosis (Yes/No).
